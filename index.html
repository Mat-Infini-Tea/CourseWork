About

On a starry night in Jerusalem on the 7th of January 1964 in a peasants small stable a beautiful baby boy was born. Minutes later 5 men arrived and introduced them self as "The 5 Wise Joes": Euan "Joe" Chree, Kaveh "Joe" Nejad, Kester "Joe" Moore, Matthew "Joe" Hutcheson, and Ryan "Joe" Stevenson.
As soon as they saw the baby's complete over acting to being cradled by his mother, they knew - that he would have an illustrious career in show-business. And with that as one Joe snatched the baby out of the mothers hands: the other Joes shot the mother in the head (as a mercy killing of course) and set the peasant's barn on fire. As they walked away with the barn burning behind them, they decided that this boy shall be named Nicolas Cage.
